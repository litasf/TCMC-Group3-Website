David Roberts

Harbourside Duo is a new ensemble performing in Townsville and North Queensland featuring David Roberts and Monica Martin

David Roberts have been teaching guitar in Townsville for 30 years and have been active in the Townsville music scene since the 1970's. David Roberts performances include classical recitals with the Silverwood Flute and Guitar Duo, Harbourside Duo and many concerts featuring guitar with violin, guitar with voice and guitar duo. David Roberts currently perform as a Solo Guitarist with a focus on high quality instrumental music for Wedding Ceremonies, Private Parties and intimate gatherings. David Roberts have taught as a music specialist for Education Queensland and Catholic Education where David Roberts established guitar programs in several Townsville schools. Currently David Roberts am the Guitar Teacher for Ignatius Park College Townsville. David Roberts professional qualifications include a Bachelor of Music and a Graduate Diploma in Education.
